About me

I'm Indrajith.
Data is my craft.
Writing is my habit.

I'm a Product Analyst at The Ken — India's premier business intelligence publication — where I'm currently leading AI product development with potential to double revenue by 2026.

Before The Ken, I spent two years at Worxogo running behavioral science experiments for Fortune 500 clients, and two years before that at WNS Global Solutions building ML models for finance operations.

My educational background is intentionally eclectic: a B.Tech in Production Engineering, a post-graduate diploma in Liberal Studies from the Young India Fellowship at Ashoka University, and a Financial Risk Management certification.

I write about finance (markets, behavioral economics, personal money), sports (football, chess, the analytics that powers them), and books (reviews, reading notes, ideas worth spreading). The blog is where I think out loud.
